# 1-Month B1 Study Plan

## Overview

This schedule is designed for 2-3 hours of study per day, 6 days a week, with a focus on the **telc Deutsch A2-B1** format.

### Week 1: Grammar Basics & Vocabulary

**Goal:** Master the "Sprachbausteine" section (30% of written exam).

| Day | Topic | Tasks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 1 | Diagnostic | Take the Diagnostic Mock Exam. Identify weak spots. |
| Day 2 | Connectors | Study ADUSO (Pos 0) vs. Inversion (Pos 1: deshalb, trotzdem). |
| Day 3 | Subordinate Clauses | Subordinating conjunctions (weil, dass, wenn, ob). **Verb to end.** |
| Day 4 | Past Tenses | Perfekt (haben vs. sein) and Modal Verbs in Präteritum. |
| Day 5 | Prepositions | Two-way prepositions (Wechselpräpositionen): Dativ vs. Akkusativ. |
| Day 6 | Adjectives | Adjective endings (Adjektivdeklination) and Comparison. |
| Day 7 | Final Push | Practice Adjective endings and Passiv basics. |

### Week 2: Reading & Listening Comprehension

**Goal:** Build speed and learn to find synonyms (Keywords).

| Day | Topic | Tasks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 8 | Reading 1 & 2 | Practice Global matching (Heads) and Detail reading (A/B/C). |
| Day 9 | Reading 3 | Match ads to situations. **Watch for negative constraints.** |
| Day 10 | Listening 1 & 2 | Radio ads and phone messages (Answering machine). |
| Day 11 | Listening 3 | Dialogues: Who said what? Practice distinguishing speakers. |
| Day 12 | Listening 4 & 5 | Interviews and opinions. Practice 'One-time only' listening. |
| Day 13 | Vocab Sprint | Focus on "Health" and "Authorities/Forms" vocab. |
| Day 14 | Review | Half Mock Exam: Reading & Listening parts only. |

### Week 3: Writing & Speaking Strategies

**Goal:** Memorize letter templates and speaking "Redemittel".

| Day | Topic | Tasks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 15 | Writing Part 1 | Informal Chat/Message templates (WhatsApp style). |
| Day 16 | Writing Part 2 | Formal Email: Job Application and Complaint templates. |
| Day 17 | Writing Part 2 | Formal Email: Apology/Request templates. Time yourself (20 min). |
| Day 18 | Speaking Part 1 | Perfect your self-introduction script (Age, Country, Job, etc.). |
| Day 19 | Speaking Part 2 | Picture description techniques (Foreground, Background, Opinion). |
| Day 20 | Speaking Part 3 | Joint Planning: Practice suggesting, agreeing, and disagreeing. |
| Day 21 | Vocab Sprint | Focus on "Work/Jobs" and "Flat/Housing" vocab. |

### Week 4: Intensive Practice & Mock Exams

**Goal:** Build exam stamina and polish weak points.

| Day | Activity | Tasks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Day 22 | Full Simulation | Take a full mock exam (uninterrupted, timed). |
| Day 23 | Review | Analyze mistakes. Focus on tricky connectors (Nebensätze). |
| Day 24 | Speaking Simulation | Record yourself speaking for all 3 parts. Review for flow. |
| Day 25 | Writing Drill | Write 3 random letters (one of each type) within 30 min. |
| Day 26 | Final Mock | The Final Mock Exam. Aim for >15 points in each section. |
| Day 27 | Polishing | Review the "Common Mistakes" guide in the Grammar module. |
| Day 28 | Vocab Review | Flashcard marathon: All A1-B1 Core Verbs and Nouns. |
| Day 29 | Relax | Light review of greetings/closings. Check your exam bag. |
| Day 30 | **EXAM DAY** | Stay calm. You have prepared well! |
