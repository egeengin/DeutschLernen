## Full Simulation
This is a full simulation. Treat it like the real exam.
- **Written Part:** 125 min.
- **Oral Part:** 15 min.

## 2. Writing Task
You see a job advertisement for a waiter in Berlin. Write a formal application (70-80 words).
- Introduce yourself.
- Describe your experience.
- Ask about working hours.

---

## 3. Speaking Task
Record yourself describing a hobby for 2 minutes.
- Why do you like it?
- How often do you do it?
- Compare it to hobbies in your home country.
