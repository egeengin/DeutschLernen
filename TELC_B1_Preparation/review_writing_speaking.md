# Review: Schreiben & Sprechen (Writing & Speaking)

These are active **output** skills. Success heavily relies on templates and confidence. 

## 1. Schreiben (Letter / Email)
You have roughly 20-30 minutes to write a letter or email (approx. 100-120 words). You must hit all 4 points given in the prompt!

### Standard Letter Format (Semi-Formal / Formal)
**Greeting:**
*   *Unknown Contact:* Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren,
*   *Known Male:* Sehr geehrter Herr [Müller],
*   *Known Female:* Sehr geehrte Frau [Schmidt],

*(Note: In Germany, start the first sentence on the next line with a lowercase letter!)*

**Introduction / Reason for Writing:**
*   ich schreibe Ihnen, weil... (I am writing to you because...)
*   hiermit möchte ich... (With this I would like to...)
*   vielen Dank für Ihre E-Mail.

**Connecting Sentences (B1 requirement):**
*   Außerdem möchte ich fragen, ob... (Furthermore I would like to ask if...)
*   Deshalb bitte ich Sie, ... (Therefore I ask you...)
*   Ich hoffe, dass... (I hope that...)

**Closing / Sign-Off:**
*   Ich freue mich auf Ihre Antwort. (I look forward to your answer.)
*   Vielen Dank im Voraus. (Thank you in advance.)
*   Mit freundlichen Grüßen (With kind regards)
*   [Your Name]

### Writing Prompt Practice:
**Situation:** You registered for a German B2 language course next week. However, you are sick and cannot attend. Write an email to the school (Frau Meier).
*Points to cover:*
1. Reason for writing.
2. Apologize.
3. Ask if you can start next month instead.
4. Ask what happens to your payment.

## 2. Sprechen (Oral Exam)
The oral exam involves introducing yourself, discussing a topic, and planning something with a partner. Interaction is key! Treat it like a ping-pong match. 

### Part 1: Introduction (Einander kennenlernen)
Have this completely memorized.
*   "Guten Tag, ich bin [Name]. Ich komme aus [Country] und lebe seit [Time] in [City]. Von Beruf bin ich [Profession]..."

### Part 2: Express Opinion on Topic/Picture
*   "Meiner Meinung nach ist das ein wichtiges Thema, weil..." (In my opinion this is an important topic because...)
*   "In meiner Heimat ist das ähnlich / anders. Bei uns..." (In my home country it is similar/different. For us...)

### Part 3: Plan Something Together (Gemeinsam etwas planen)
You and a partner need to plan an activity (e.g., buying a gift, organizing a party).
**Making Suggestions:**
*   "Was denkst du? Sollen wir vielleicht...?" (What do you think? Should we perhaps...?)
*   "Wie wäre es, wenn wir...?" (How about if we...?)
**Agreeing/Disagreeing:**
*   "Das ist eine super Idee!" (That is a great idea!)
*   "Ich bin mir nicht sicher. Vielleicht ist es besser, wenn..." (I am not sure. Maybe it's better if...)

### Speaking Practice Prompt:
**Situation (Part 3):** Your colleague is leaving the company next Friday. You and your partner want to organize a farewell gift and a small party in the office. Discuss: When to buy the gift, what to buy, who buys food and drinks, when the party starts. 
*(Practice roleplaying this conversation aloud!)*
