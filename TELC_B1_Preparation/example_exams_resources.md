# Resources & Links

## Official Sources
- **telc Official Practice Tests:** [telc.net/practice-exams](https://www.telc.net/en/candidates/language-examinations/tests/telc-deutsch-a2b1.html)
- **A2/B1 Manual (Handbuch):** Detailed scoring rules from telc.

## Video Courses
- **JudiAegi Schule (YouTube):** Best channel for real telc exam walkthroughs.
- **Deutsch Lera:** Focuses on speaking and grammar reaction phrases.
- **Learn German with Ania:** Fun and engaging A1-B1 grammar reviews.

## Digital Learning
- **DW — Nicos Weg (B1):** Interactive video-based story course by Deutsche Welle.
- **Lingolia:** Comprehensive German grammar explanations and charts.
- **Duden:** The standard German dictionary for spelling and grammar.

## Mobile Apps
- **Anki / Quizlet:** Recommended for space-repetition vocabulary learning.
- **HelloTalk / Tandem:** Language exchange apps for speaking practice with natives.
