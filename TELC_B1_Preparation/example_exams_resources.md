## How to Use These Resources in Your 30-Day Plan

| Week       | Recommended Resources                                                    |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Week 1** | Lingolia grammar exercises (Nebensätze, Konnektoren, Perfekt)            |
| **Week 2** | DW Top-Thema daily; G.A.S.T. Übungssatz Lesen section                    |
| **Week 3** | DW Langsam gesprochene Nachrichten; YouTube Hören practice tests         |
| **Week 4** | Full official TELC / Goethe / G.A.S.T. mock exams under timed conditions |

> **Tip:** Do the G.A.S.T. Übungssatz 1 and the TELC official Modelltest on separate weeks —
> they are your two best real-format practice exams.
